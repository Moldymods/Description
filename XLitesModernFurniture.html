How to Acquire Furniture In Survival 

To begin with you will need the Furniture table

To craft the Furniture Table you will need the Items Shown Below

  • 5 Iron Ingots
  • 3 Wooden Slabs
  • 1 Leather

Once inside the Furniture Table You'll be able to make a multitude of Items
The Main Things to create Furniture are Blueprints and then Furniture Crates

                                                                                                        

Once you have Made your furniture crate take it to the stone cutter to create Furniture!
