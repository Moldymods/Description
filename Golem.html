Golem's+

Hello, guys this time I bring you an addon that adds craftable golems, 16 in total with different lives and abilitiesWith these golems you can defend your village with any type of golem you want to craft.

Golems:

Iron Golem

This golem is the normal one that we know in minecraft, it does not have any ability, it will only come in spawn and can be crafted.

Craftings

(Just like making a golem but at the crafting table with 3 iron blocks and a pumpkin,repeat this with other golems but with their corresponding materials.)
